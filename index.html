PERSONAL DETAILS

SABO

Sabo is the chief of staff of the Revolutionary Army, recognized as "No. 2" of the entire organization, ranking directly below Monkey D. Dragon.

Summary

At a very young age, Sabo ran away from his noble parents in the Goa Kingdom to live alone in the Gray Terminal, kingdom, and he met and befriended Ace not long after.

Five years later (12 before the current storyline), the two became friends with Luffy as well, and the three consider themselves "brothers", swearing a brotherhood for sake. They also share a dream of setting out to sea as pirates and achieving freedom, Sabo originally planned to do so at the age of 17.

However, after escaping his parents once again not long after they captured him and took him away from his adopted brother, Sabo then set sail on his own, but his ship was destroyed by a Heavenly Dragon moments later.

Because he was believed to have died during this incident, Sabo actually survived because Dragon saved him, and thus he joined the Revolutionaries. He has lost most of his memories since then but returned after learning of Ace's death at Marineford. He currently has a bounty of 602,000,000 Berries.

Education

  • As a child, Sabo went through his education with Ace and Luffy.
  • After that he trained with the revolutionary army under the leadership of Monkey D. Dragon.
  • Sabo continued his education and training after eating the Mera Mera devil fruit which he got from Dressrosa, which at that time was controlled by Doffy (Donquixote Doflamingo).

Work experience

Sabo was often involved in various quite dangerous missions by the Revolutionary troops, including:

  1. Declaration of war on Tenryuubito.
  2. Rescue of slaves, including Kuma.
  3. Destroy enemy food buildings.

Skills

  1. Great fighting ability.
  2. Strategy.
  3. Haki
  4. Mera Mera no mi
